UALBANY ASSOCIATION FOR COMPUTING MACHINERY

What We Do

This is the student chapter of ACM at the University at SUNY Albany. ACM consists of some of the best leaders in the computer science department. We run workshops, collaborative coding nights, study sessions, and we aim to provide help and guidance to the students in the College of Computing and Information. We are working to bridge the gaps between the different departments in college. We plan to provide many informational and social event opportunities as well as anything else that may be in demand. We want to strengthen the learning community in CEAS, and make it more peer-based.
